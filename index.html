What Can I Do For You?

I will be offering people a 6 weeks minimum, specific plan where you can effectively change your life to become the happiest, healthiest and most effective person you can be.

I will provide you with strict, tailored exercise plans and an app that will record all of your data so we can work on things, discuss the exercise plans and change them according to what you enjoy or what we feel is working best for you.

I will provide you with low budget diet sheets with great food and quick easy meals that will make you feel great. You will track what you're eating and drinking in the app provided so that I can monitor your intake and make sure it matches exactly the goals you are aiming for in order to see the best results.

There will be regular weekly calls with myself to discuss your fitness and health goals, what you're wanting to achieve from the program, and how you are finding everything I offer. This will be a chance to ask questions and let me know if there is any parts of the program that you prefer or what you may be struggling with, or even just to catch up with each other to discuss life and other things.

I will provide a document with easy life habits and unspoken rules that people have forgotten about that will make you feel amazing!
